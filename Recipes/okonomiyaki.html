Okonomiyaki

Description:

Okonomiyaki is a Japanese dish that can be described as "grilled stuffed pancake." Unlike Western breakfast-style pancakes, okonomiyaki is a savory dish, filled with a variety of veggies and meats and often topped with special okonomiyaki sauce, Japenese mayo, and panko.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 4 cups chopped cabbage
  • 6 strips bacon, crumbled
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 sausage, diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/4 cup cooked shrimp
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup tenkasu
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Toppings:

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 1 tbsppanko bread crumbs
  • 1 tsp mayonaise

Steps:

  1. Mix flour and water together in a bowl until smooth. Stir in cabbage, bacon, eggs, sausage, green onions, shrimp, cheese, and tenkasu.
  2. Preheat a griddle to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C); coat with oil. Pour in flour mixture in the shape of a pancake. Cook until golden brown, about 6 minutes per side. Transfer to a serving plate.
  3. Mix soy sauce, ketchup, and vinegar together in a small bowl to make okonomiyaki sauce. Drizzle over pancake. Garnish with panko and mayonnaise.
